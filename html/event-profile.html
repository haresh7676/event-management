About

Quirktastic Inc is pleased to announce QuirkCon, as well as the 1st Annual Cosplayer Awards to take place in downtown Durham at the Durham Convention Center (301 W. Morgan St.) on Friday, May 31, 2019 to Sunday, June 2, 2019.

QuirkCon is a 3 day conference for geeks, gamers and nerds, with a primary focus on people of color and the LGBT+ community.

Topics covered at this conference include anime, cosplay, comic books, gaming, sci-fi, art, film, mental health and alternative lifestyles.
